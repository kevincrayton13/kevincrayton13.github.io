Craig Reynold's Boids

Instructions

      The program models a flock of birds, and how they will slowly regather themselves after being disturbed. The user may add a 'boid' by left clicking anywhere in the chart, and create a disturbance (so the 'boids' separate) by right clicking a location.

Executable File (The program will need a second to load everything, no need to type anything)

Additional Information

      Boids is an artificial life program developed by Craig Reynolds. It has three simple rules. 1: The Boids (Birds with a New York accent) must be separated by a certain amount. 2: The Boids must seek to align with each other. 3: The Boids must go towards the average position of their flockmates. The additional rule included in the program is they must avoid the position that is right clicked, simulating a predator or some other threat.
